Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our pharmacy services, prescriptions, and health guidance.

1. Do I need a prescription to get medicines from your pharmacy?

Some medicines require a valid prescription from a licensed healthcare provider, while others are available over the counter. Our pharmacists can guide you on what is needed.

2. Do you offer prescription refills?

Yes, we provide prescription refill services. You can request refills in person or through our online/phone services, depending on availability.

3. How long does it take to fill a prescription?

Most prescriptions are filled within 15–30 minutes. Complex prescriptions or unavailable medicines may take longer.

4. Can your pharmacist explain how to take my medication?

Absolutely. Our pharmacists are happy to explain dosage instructions, possible side effects, and answer any questions you may have.

6. Do you provide medication counseling?

Yes, we offer confidential medication counseling to help you understand your treatment and use medicines safely.

9. Do you offer home delivery services?

Many pharmacies offer home delivery for prescriptions and health products. Please contact us to confirm availability and delivery areas.
